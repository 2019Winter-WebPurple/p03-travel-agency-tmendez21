Grenada

Grenada is a country in the Caribbean consisting of one main island and several smaller islands. Its capital and largest city is St. George's. There are several things to do and plenty of tourist attractions to see. The climate is beautiful and stays between 70 and 80 degrees Fahrenheit year-round. The history of Grenada is extremely interesting, and there are several historic sites that you can go see! The food in Grenada is delicious and has a lot of variety, so there is something for everyone to eat. Finally, Grenada is most well-known for its beautiful beaches, the most popular being Grand Anse Beach.

What To Do in Grenada

If you like to stay busy, then Grenada is the place for you! There are several things to see and do! Here are just a few of them:

Fort George
A fort that was built in the early 1700s and has an extremely rich history.

Tutti Frutti Island
An island tour with pretty views, agriculture, rainforests, coasts, and a quality lunch.

Sculpture Snorkle and Powerboat Sightseeing
An underwateradventure on a guided speedboat tour around the Caribbean Sea.

Coastline ATV Adventure
An adventurous tour taken on ATVs that takes you along the coasts of Grenada.

Molinere Underwater Sculpture Park
An underwater attraction where you can find one-of-a-kind sculptures off the west coast of Grenada in the Caribbean Sea.

Annandale Falls
A very popular and beautiful waterfall that is visited by many each year.
